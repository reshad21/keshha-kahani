ABOUT ME

Why i write

Your Literary Community

I have always had a strong, intrinsic urge to put pen to paper. Crafting poetry and prose allows me to channel my creative energy while also providing a framework for processing my innermost thoughts and emotions.I began publishing my writing online with the hope that it would help other budding writers develop their voices. Today, in addition to being an outlet for my own work, KISA KAHANI includes poetry, moral lesson stories and more. I hope that the site is useful and inspirational to you on your literary journey.
